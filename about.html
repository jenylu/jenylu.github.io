Hello there! I'm Jennifer.

I'm currently a third year undergraduate studying computer science at UC San Diego. I am from the Bay Area, born in Palo Alto and raised in Cupertino. I decided to pursue computer science after taking an intro class during winter quarter of my freshman year because I loved being able to make things. Computer science challenges me to problem solve and think differently.

Experience

Last summer, I was an intern at Microsoft as part of the Bing Core UX team. I created an internal testing tool that would allow developers/testers to compare how a feature they created would look across different browsers and devices. I am also part of the outreach committee for Women In Computing where I am planning 'Girl's Day Out', an event to encourage local high school girls to join the STEM field. Currently, I am a tutor for the CSE Department for CSE 11, Introduction to Computer Science and Object-Oriented Programming: Java.

Personal Interests

In my free time, I like sketching and painting. I also enjoy swimming, playing piano, and nail art. I am always up for trying new things!
