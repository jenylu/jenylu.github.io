CSE 110: Software Engineering

VINO | Android app

Spring 2013

With a team of ten, I developed a wine diary application for wine lovers as part of the Software Engineering course. We created a wine diary that records visits to wineries and details about wine tasted, as well as favorites and recommendations based on the user's most liked wines. I had the role of Senior System Analyst where I researched any problems we had, planned solutions, and performed system testing. Our team worked in a waterfall development cycle. We learned how to write use cases, user stories, screen sequence diagrams, and do version control with github. I had to learn many new applications for this course and it was a huge learning experience where we were given a lot of freedom to do what we wanted with the assignment.
