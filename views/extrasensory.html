ExtraSensory REU: Context-Recognition Application using Mobile Sensors

Date: Oct 2014- June 2015

Under the REU (Research Experience for Undergraduates) program, I worked in Gert Lanckriet's lab under two of his PhD students. The ultimate goal was to create a mobile application that would predict the actions, mood, and setting of a user based on mobile sensors like GPS, speedometer, and motion detection.

To do this, I worked on the iOS and Android mobile application, ExtraSensory to collect a rich data set in order to improve the context-recognition algorithm.
